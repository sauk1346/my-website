## Lecture - Greeting

<audio controls style={{width:'100%'}}>
    <source src="/idiomas/chino/audio/greeting.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"/>
</audio>

1. **nǐ hǎo \[ní hǎo\]:** hello
2. **Wǒ jiào Tínɡnà:** My name is Tínɡnà 
3. **Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?:** What's your name?
4. **Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ:** Nice to meet you
