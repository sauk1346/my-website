|Hanzi|Pinyin|Meaning|
|---|---|---|
|旭东 你多大了?|Xùdōng, nǐ duō dà le?|Xùdōng, how old are you?|
|我二十四岁 你呢?|Wǒ èr shí sì suì. Nǐ ne?|I'm 24 years old, and you?|
|我二十一岁 他多大了?|Wǒ èr shí yi suì. Tā duǒ dà le?|I'm 21 years old. How old is he?|
|他十八岁 |Tā shí bā suì.|He is 18 years old|

### Particle

Topic + ne

Particle **ne** can be used to ask reciprocal questions, also know as bouce-back question (how about you?)
