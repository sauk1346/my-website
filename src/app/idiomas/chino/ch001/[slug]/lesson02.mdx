## Lecture

1. **Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi:** What's your name?
2. **Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?**: What's your nationality? / Where are you from?
3. **Wǒ shì Zhōnɡɡuó rén:** I'm chinese
4. **Nǐ shì Měiɡuó rén:** You are american
5. **Tā shì Yìdàlì rén:** She is italian
6. **Tā yě shì Zhōnɡɡuó rén:** She is also chinese
