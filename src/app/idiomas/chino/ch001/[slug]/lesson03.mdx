### Conversation

    <source src="/idiomas/chino/audio/conversation02.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"/>
</audio>

|Pinyin|Meaning|
|---|---|
|Xùdōng, nǐ duō dà le?|Xùdōng, how old are you?|
|Wǒ èr shí sì suì. Nǐ ne?|I'm 24 years old, and you?|
|Wǒ èr shí yi suì. Tā duǒ dà le?|I'm 21 years old. How old is she?|
|Tā shí bā suì.|She is 18 years old|

### Particle

Topic + ne

Particle **ne** can be used to ask reciprocal questions, also know as bouce-back question (how about you?)
