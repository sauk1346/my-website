## Phonemic symbols
Special symbols that represent sounds

- **jump**. Starts with /ʤ/
- **chalk**. Starts with /ʧ/
- **this**. Starts with /ð/
- **thick**. Starts with /θ/
- **show**. Starts with /ʃ/
- **vision**. Has sound in the middle /ʒ/
- **song**. Ends with /ŋ/
