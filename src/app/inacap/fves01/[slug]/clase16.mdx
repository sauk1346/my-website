# Role-play Exercises

## 1. Role-play Plan

- 3 Ice breaker questions/anwers
- 4 Questions/answers (Will/Going to)
- 1 Question/answer (Would you like to)
- 1 Question/answer (Present-continous-progressive)
- 4 Good bye chunks
