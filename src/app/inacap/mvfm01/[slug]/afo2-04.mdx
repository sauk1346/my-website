1. **Determine el valor del segmento $\overline{AB}$**

<Solution>
1. Se calcula el ángulo faltante

<SmartFigure
	src="/inacap/mvfm01/images/fig165.png"
	alt="Cálculo ángulo faltante"
	caption="Cálculo ángulo faltante"
	size="large"
/>

$\angle x = 78°$

2. Se aplica la ley de los senos

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \frac{a}{\sin(\alpha)} &&= \frac{b}{\sin(\beta)} &&= \frac {c}{\sin(\gamma)}\\[15pt]
    \frac{11.12}{\sin(65)} &&= \frac{b}{\sin(37)} &&= \frac {c}{\sin(78)}
\end{aligned}
$$

Se tiene que: $\overline{AB} = c$

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \frac{11.12}{\sin(65)} &= \frac {c}{\sin(78)}
\end{aligned}
$$

<SmartFigure
	src="/inacap/mvfm01/images/fig166.png"
	alt="Cálculo segmento AB"
	caption="Cálculo segmento AB"
	size="large"
/>

El segmento $\overline{AB}$ mide `12` unidades

</Solution>

\frac{11.12}{\sin(65)} &= \frac{b}{\sin(37)}
\end{aligned}
$$
